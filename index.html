Welcome to Brewing Ideas!!

this is an all-encompassing podcast where your hosts (Ashrita and Yamila) sit down with a fresh pot of tea and a never-ending supply of curiousity in the big city! We are so excited to finally be starting this long-awaited project with everyone:) From the profound to the mundane, we'll brew up conversations on everything under the sun. We'll delve into the big questions, the small moments, and everything in between, so grab a cup, get comfy, and let's brew some ideas together!
